Hampi, Karnataka

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its ancient temples, ruins, and magnificent stone architecture. It was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire and is now one of the most iconic historical sites in India.

Red Fort, Delhi

Constructed in the 17th century by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, the Red Fort is a symbol of India's rich history and a must-visit heritage site in the heart of Delhi.

Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

These rock-cut caves are among the finest examples of ancient Indian art, architecture, and religious history, featuring detailed sculptures and murals from Buddhism, Hinduism, and Jainism.
